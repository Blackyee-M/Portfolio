Echoes Of Her

Core Concept

You take on the role of a powerful Witch with time-rewinding abilities. She can manipulate time to destroy or restore what’s broken. Through these powers, she navigates a fractured world in search of the memories of a loved one lost to the collapse of time.


How it Started:

The idea for Echo's Of Her started with a mechanic I once scrapped back in college — time rewinding. I had built it purely as a feature, but it always felt like it deserved more. I wanted to give it meaning. That’s when the story began to form — a witch who rewinds time not just to alter the world, but to search for what she’s lost, to question what she’s gained, and to confront where everything went wrong. It became more than a mechanic; it became a way to explore regret, purpose, and the weight of trying to fix what may not be fixable.
